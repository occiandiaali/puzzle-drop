About PuzzleDrop

PuzzleDrop is a free, browser-based jigsaw puzzle game that lets you turn any personal photo into a fully playable puzzle — no apps, no installs required.

Simply upload an image, choose your difficulty and piece shape, then drag and drop the pieces until the picture is complete. Race against the timer and challenge yourself!

Features:

  • Upload any JPEG or PNG image
  • Rectangular or irregular jigsaw piece shapes
  • 4 difficulty levels (9 to 36 pieces)
  • Live timer and piece counter
  • Confetti celebration when you win 🎉
  • Fully mobile-friendly drag-and-drop

All image processing is done entirely in your browser — your photos are never uploaded to any server.
